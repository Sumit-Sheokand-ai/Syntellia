## 🎓 DESIGN PRINCIPLES APPLIED

### 1. **Progressive Disclosure**
Information reveals as user scrolls:
- Hero: Core value proposition
- Story: Why it matters (education)
- Tools: What you can do (action)
- Features: Why choose us (conversion)

### 2. **Visual Hierarchy**
Size and color guide attention:
- Largest: Hero title (action)
- Gradients: Important headings
- Muted: Supporting text
- Colored: Interactive elements

### 3. **F-Pattern Reading**
Layout follows eye movement:
- Top-left: Logo
- Top-right: Actions (Login/Signup)
- Center-top: Main headline
- Left-to-right: Timeline flow

### 4. **80/20 Rule**
- 80% dark background (calm)
- 20% colorful accents (energy)
- Keeps focus without overwhelming

### 5. **Gestalt Principles**
- **Proximity**: Related items grouped
- **Similarity**: Consistent card design
- **Continuity**: Timeline connection
- **Figure/Ground**: Clear separation
